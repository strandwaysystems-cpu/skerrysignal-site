Common questions

Straight answers before we talk

The questions every contractor asks. No fine print.

How is this different from HomeStars, Bark, or buying leads online?
Those platforms sell the same lead to three, four, five contractors at once — so you're racing competitors on every call. Our leads are exclusive: one business per city, per trade. The homeowner reaches you, and only you.
What does it cost?
It depends on your trade and market, and we keep it simple — either a flat monthly fee or a per-lead price, agreed up front. Founding partners get early pricing locked in. We'll give you exact numbers on the 15-minute call once we've looked at your market.
What if I don't get any leads?
You're month-to-month, never locked into a long contract. If the leads aren't turning into booked jobs, you're free to walk. We only keep clients by earning it every month.
Are the leads any good, or just tire-kickers?
Every lead is pre-qualified by AI before it reaches you — service type, timeline, location, and whether they actually own the home. You get a full call recording with each one, so you can hear the lead for yourself.
Do I have to be a big company?
No — the opposite. We're built for owner-operators and small teams across Atlantic Canada who are great at the trade but don't have time to chase marketing. That's exactly who we work with.
